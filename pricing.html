Questions

Good answers to fair questions

Can I cancel my Pro membership anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account settings with no penalties or hidden fees. Your access continues through the end of your billing period.
What's included in the AI chat assistant? ▼
Pro members can ask the AI anything career-related — how to phrase a bullet point, whether to include certain experience, how to tailor their resume for a specific job posting, interview tips, and more. It's like having a career coach on demand.
What happens during the consultation session? ▼
We'll do a 60-minute video or phone call where I interview you about your background, goals, and target roles. I'll ask questions you've probably never been asked — then write your resume personally within 48 hours. You get one round of revisions included.
Is the Single Resume really a one-time charge? ▼
Yes — pay once, get your resume. No subscription starts, no auto-renew, no tricks. If you want to edit it later or build a new one, you'd pay again or upgrade to Pro.
How does the annual billing discount work? ▼
Choosing annual billing locks in a 20% discount — you pay $192/year instead of $240. That's two months free. It's billed as a single charge upfront.
What format does my resume come in? ▼
The AI generates your resume as clean, formatted text you can copy directly into Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or any other editor to finalize the layout and styling. Formatted PDF export is coming soon.
